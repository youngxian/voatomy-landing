# Voatomy Pricing Strategy
## by Voatomy Labs

> **"Start free. Scale as you grow. No hidden fees. No surprises."**

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Voatomy pricing is designed around three principles:

1. **Land and Expand** — Start with ATLAS free tier, add products as needs grow
2. **Transparent** — No hidden fees, no surprise overages, clear feature boundaries
3. **Value-Aligned** — Pay for the intelligence you use, not seats you don't

## Annual vs. Monthly

- **Annual billing**: ~17% discount
- **Monthly billing**: Full flexibility, cancel anytime
- All plans include 14-day free trial of paid features

## FAQ Summary

1. **Start with one product, add more?** — Yes, mix and match anytime
2. **What counts as a user?** — Anyone who logs in and uses the platform
3. **Startup discounts?** — Yes, 50% off first year for qualifying startups
4. **Free plan limits?** — 1 team, 1 repo, 2 AI plans/month (no credit card required)
5. **Switch plans?** — Anytime, pro-rated billing
6. **Minimum commitment?** — None for monthly, annual requires 12 months
7. **Non-profit pricing?** — 30% discount for registered non-profits
8. **Payment methods?** — All major credit cards, ACH, wire transfer (Enterprise)
