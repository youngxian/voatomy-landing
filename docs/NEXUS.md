## Core Capabilities

### 1. Unified Search (⌘K)
One search bar across all products and external tools:
- Cross-product results: query "Auth SSO" and see LOOP features, ATLAS tickets, PHANTOM debt, SIGNAL incidents, and DRIFT components
- Context-aware ranking based on current product
- Natural language queries: "which features are blocking more than $1M in pipeline?"
- Command actions: "create ticket", "push to jira", "export report"
- < 200ms P95 latency

### 2. Executive Dashboard
Organization-wide visibility for leadership:
- Cross-team health scores
- Revenue pipeline alignment with engineering delivery
- Velocity trending across all teams
- Organization-wide tech debt trajectory
- Design system health and consistency

### 3. Cross-Product Intelligence
Intelligence that no single product can generate alone:
- Sprint plans informed by revenue urgency (ATLAS + LOOP)
- Tech debt prioritized by incident frequency (PHANTOM + SIGNAL)
- Design changes correlated with revenue impact (DRIFT + LOOP)
- Incident response informed by sprint commitments (SIGNAL + ATLAS)

### 4. Organization-Wide Metrics
Aggregate metrics across the entire organization:
- Engineering efficiency score
- Revenue-to-delivery alignment
- Cross-team dependency health
- Innovation vs. maintenance ratio
- Customer satisfaction correlation
