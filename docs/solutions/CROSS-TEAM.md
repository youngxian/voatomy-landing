# Use Case: Cross-Team Alignment
## by Voatomy Labs

> **"Connect engineering, design, product, sales"**

---

## Problem
Teams operate in silos. Engineering ships without knowing revenue impact. Sales promises without knowing engineering capacity. Design creates without adoption data. Product prioritizes without demand signals. The result: $1 Trillion+ lost annually to cross-functional misalignment.

## Solution
Voatomy products bridge every team through shared intelligence — sprint plans informed by revenue, designs connected to adoption data, incidents routed with business context, and feedback flowing from customers to engineering and back.

## Products Used
All Voatomy products contribute to cross-team alignment:

| Connection | Products | What Flows |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| Engineering ↔ Product | ATLAS + LOOP | Sprint plans informed by revenue priorities |
| Sales ↔ Engineering | LOOP | Feature demand signals, ship notifications |
| Design ↔ Engineering | DRIFT | Token sync, component adoption, drift detection |
| SRE ↔ CS ↔ Sales | SIGNAL | Incident context routed to right teams |
| Everyone | NEXUS | Unified search, executive dashboards |
