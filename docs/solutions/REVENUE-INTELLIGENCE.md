# Use Case: Revenue Intelligence
## by Voatomy Labs

> **"Link customer feedback to business outcomes"**

---

## Problem
73% of product decisions lack revenue data. Companies lose an average of 23% of pipeline to feature gaps they didn't know existed. The disconnect between customer demand and engineering delivery costs organizations $1 Trillion+ annually.

## Solution
LOOP + ATLAS create a revenue intelligence layer that captures customer signals, weights them by dollar value, feeds them into sprint prioritization, and tracks revenue attribution from signal to ship.

## Primary Products
- **LOOP** (#6366F1) — Revenue signal capture, demand scoring, attribution
- **ATLAS** (#f16e2c) — Revenue-weighted sprint planning

## Revenue Signal Sources
| Source | Tool | Intelligence |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Sales calls | Gong | Feature demand extraction, competitive mentions |
| Support tickets | Zendesk | Churn risk detection, demand quantification |
| CRM pipeline | Salesforce | Pipeline impact per feature, deal urgency |
| Customer surveys | NPS/CSAT | Satisfaction correlation, theme clustering |

## Revenue-to-Sprint Connection
1. LOOP identifies "$2.3M blocked on SSO" from 47 accounts
2. ATLAS prioritizes SSO in Sprint 26 based on revenue weight
3. Engineering ships SSO in Sprint 28
4. LOOP notifies 47 requesting accounts
5. Sales closes 12 deals = $1.4M influenced revenue
