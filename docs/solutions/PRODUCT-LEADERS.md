# Solution: Product Leaders
## by Voatomy Labs

> **"Revenue-weighted backlogs, customer signal alignment"**

---

## Target Audience
VP Product, Product Managers, Head of Product, CPO

## Primary Pain Points
1. Customer feedback goes into a black hole — no structured capture
2. No way to quantify feature demand in revenue dollars
3. Sales and engineering speak different languages
4. Shipped features don't get communicated back to customers who requested them

## Recommended Voatomy Stack

| Product | Role | Priority |
|---------|------|----------|
| **LOOP** | Revenue feedback engine, signal aggregation, demand scoring | Primary |
| **ATLAS** | Sprint planning visibility, delivery tracking | Secondary |
| **DRIFT** | Design system adoption, UX analytics | Supporting |

## Key Value Propositions
- Revenue-weighted backlog: every feature carries a dollar value
- Signal aggregation from Gong, Zendesk, Salesforce, NPS
- Auto-generated sales enablement when features ship
- Close the loop: customers who requested features get notified
- Cross-team visibility into engineering velocity and design scope

## Revenue Feedback Loop
1. Customer says "We need SSO" → LOOP captures $2.3M pipeline demand
2. PM prioritizes in revenue-weighted backlog
3. Engineering ships in Sprint 26
4. LOOP auto-generates: sales brief, CS talking points, marketing copy
5. Customer gets notified → renewal secured

## Key Metrics
- Revenue influenced per quarter: $4.2M
- Signal-to-ship cycle time: < 2 sprints
- Deals accelerated per feature ship: 7
- Cross-team alignment score: 92%
