## Overview

SIGNAL is Voatomy's incident intelligence platform — automatically translating technical incidents into business impact and routing the right context to every team. When a P1 hits, SRE sees technical blast radius, CS sees affected accounts, Sales sees at-risk deals, and leadership sees revenue exposure — all within 60 seconds.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Revenue-Aware Impact Assessment
Instantly translate incidents into business terms:
- Map affected services → affected customers → ARR at risk
- Calculate revenue exposure in real-time
- Correlate incident severity with business priority
- Historical pattern matching for impact prediction

### 2. Intelligent Context Routing
Different teams need different context — SIGNAL delivers it automatically:

| Team | Context Delivered |
|------|-------------------|
| **SRE** | Technical blast radius, related alerts, runbooks, service dependencies |
| **Customer Success** | Affected accounts, customer-facing talking points, status templates |
| **Sales** | At-risk deals, pipeline impact, hold/proceed recommendations |
| **Leadership** | Revenue exposure, customer count, resolution ETA, board-ready summary |

### 3. Incident Timeline Intelligence
Automated timeline construction:
- Alert triggered → Services identified → Impact calculated → Teams notified → Resolution → Post-mortem auto-generated
- Full audit trail with decision points
- SLA tracking and breach prediction

### 4. Post-Mortem Automation
AI-generated post-mortem reports:
- Root cause analysis assistance
- Impact summary (technical + business)
- Action items extraction
- Follow-up ticket creation
- Trend analysis across incidents
